# RevInsight AI Dashboard

## Project Overview
RevInsight AI is an end-to-end Power BI project designed to analyze business performance across revenue, profitability, channels, categories, and cities.

The dashboard provides:
- Executive business overview
- Sales intelligence insights
- AI-driven forecasting and risk analysis

---

## Problem Statement
Businesses often struggle to identify:
- Revenue trends
- Top-performing channels
- High-risk categories
- Forecasted growth opportunities

This dashboard solves these problems using interactive analytics and AI-based insights.

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Dashboard
Key business KPIs and performance overview.

KPIs:
- Total Revenue
- Orders
- Net Profit
- AOV
- Return Rate
- Profit Margin

Visuals:
- Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Channel
- Revenue by Category
- Revenue by City
- Profit Margin Trend

---

### 2. Sales Intelligence Dashboard
Deep dive into category performance and profitability.

KPIs:
- Top Category
- Top Category Revenue
- Worst Category
- Best Channel
- Worst Category Revenue

Visuals:
- Return Rate by Category
- Profit by Category
- Category Trend
- Profit Breakdown

---

### 3. AI Insights Dashboard
Advanced forecasting and AI-driven recommendations.

KPIs:
- Forecast Revenue
- Revenue Growth
- AI Risk Score
- Alert Severity

Visuals:
- AI Risk Heatmap
- Forecast vs Actual Revenue
- AI Summary
- Recommendation Engine

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI

- Excel

## Key Insights
- Quick Commerce emerged as top-performing channel.
- Jaipur generated highest revenue.
- Fashion category delivered strongest performance.
- Beauty category showed higher risk signals.
- Forecast indicates 6% revenue growth.
